How It Works

Four steps. Zero fraud.

A seamless flow for students, full control for lecturers — impossible to fake at every stage.

01

Lecturer Starts a Session

The lecturer opens the app and taps Start — a live QR code is instantly displayed on screen for the class.

Ready in under 3 seconds.
02

Student Logs In & Authenticates

Students open their dedicated app, enter their email and password, then complete face authentication to verify their identity before proceeding.

Biometric identity confirmed.
03

Scan QR & Connect to Beacon

The student scans the displayed QR code. This automatically connects them to the classroom Bluetooth beacon, instantly confirming they are physically in the room.

Room presence confirmed.
04

Monitored Continuously, Displayed Live

The Bluetooth beacon runs ongoing background checks throughout the session to confirm the student stays in the room. As each check-in is confirmed, attendance updates in real time on the monitor — visible to the lecturer the instant it happens.

Live presence. Live dashboard.
